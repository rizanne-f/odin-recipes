Odin Recipes

  • Adobo

    Filipino adobo is a tangy, savory stew of meat simmered in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns, creating a rich, aromatic flavor.

  • Sinigang

    Filipino sinigang is a sour, savory soup made with meat or seafood simmered in tamarind broth, tomatoes, and vegetables, creating a refreshing tang.

  • Nilaga

    Filipino nilaga is a clear, comforting soup made with boiled meat, usually beef or pork, and vegetables like corn, potatoes, and saba bananas.
